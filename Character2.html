Nico di Angelo

Basic Facts:

Birthday: January 28, 1924

Age: 14(Biologically) or 82(Chronologically)

Species: Demigod

Titles:

  • Son of Hades
  • The Ghost King
  • Ambassador of Hades
  • Survivor of Tartarus

Items: Stygian Iron Sword

Backstory:

Nico di Angelo lived in Italy until Zeus, the god of the Sky, kills his mother. His father Hades, the god of the dead takes him and his older sister, Bianca di Angelo, to the Lotus Casino, which defies the laws of time, and they unknowingly stay there for 70 years. They then eventually get to Camp Half-Blood, with Bianca now as one of the immortal Hunters of Artemis. His sister goes on a quest and dies on the way. When Nico finds out he runs away and figures out he is a son of Hades from the Ghost of King Minos, who betrays him and Nico become the Ghost King.
