Heroes of Olympus by Rick Riordan

About the Series:

The Heroes of Olympus is a series based on modern Greek and Roman mythology. There is a prophecy that states 'Seven halfbloods shall answer the call, To storm or fire, the world must fall. An oath to keep with the final breath, And foes bear arms at the doors of death.

In book one ,The Lost Hero, three demigods by the name of Jason Grace, Piper Mclean, and Leo Valdez are taken to the greek demigod trainimg camp called Camp Half-Blood. While there, the Oracle of Delphi, Rachel Dare, says a prophesy which takes them on a quest to rescue the Queen of the Gods, Hera, from one of the giant sons of Gaia. On the way they encounter many obstacles, which slow down their journey.

In book two, The Son of Neptune, a demigod named Percy Jackson has lost his memory and finds his way to the Roman training Camp called Camp Jupiter where he befriends Hazel Levesque and Frank Zhang. They find themselves on a quest to Alaska to rescue the god of death, Thatnos. Along the way, they face many challenges, but they figure out a way to get through them all using their skills and working together. When they come back, Percy's memory is completely restored and they face a battle against one of the giants and his memory.

In book three, The Mark of Athena, the heroes from Camp Half-Blood, Annabeth Chase, Leo Valdez, Piper Mclean, and Jason Grace fly to Camp Jupiter on the Argo II and pick up the demigods Percy Jackson, Hazel Levesque, and Frank Zhang. They fly to Europe to rescue Nico di Angelo and for Annabeth to do her quest on following the Mark of Athena
