Basic Facts:

Age: 16

Backstory:

Leo Valdez is an orphan from the age of 8 when his mom dies in a fire. He has been running away from each foster home he is taken into and eventually goes to the Wilderness School for troubled kids. There he meets Piper Mclean and Jason Grace, his best friends
