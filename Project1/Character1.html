Percy Jackson

Basic Facts:

Birthday: August 18, 1993

Age: 17

Species: Demigod

Titles:

  • Son of Poseidon
  • Savior of Olympus (Twice)
  • Survivor of Tartarus

Items: Riptide (Celestial Bronze Sword)

Backstory:

Percy Jackson finds out he is the demigod son of Poseidon at age 12 when he makes his way to Camp Halfblood and has his first quest to find the King of the Gods weapon called the Master Bolt. From age 12-16, he participates in many adventures in the series of Percy Jackson and the Olympians.
